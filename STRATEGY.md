# Long-Term ULIP Investment Strategy (25 Years)

## Objective
Maximize long-term wealth (₹1–2 crore goal).

---

## Phase 1 – High Growth (Years 0–10)
- 60% Discovery Fund
- 20% Diversified Equity
- 20% Flexi Cap

**Goal:** Maximize compounding early.

---

## Phase 2 – Growth + Stability (Years 11–15)
- 40% Discovery
- 20% Diversified Equity
- 20% Flexi Cap
- 20% Equity Advantage

**Goal:** Reduce pure midcap exposure & add bluechip.

---

## Phase 3 – Protect Gains (Years 16–20)
- 20% Discovery
- 20% Diversified Equity
- 20% Flexi Cap
- 20% Equity Advantage
- 20% Bond/Secure Fund

**Goal:** Reduce risk as amount grows.

---

## Phase 4 – Maturity Safety (Years 21–25)
- 10% Discovery
- 20% Diversified
- 20% Flexi
- 20% Equity Advantage
- 30% Bond/Secure

**Goal:** Protect from final-year volatility.
